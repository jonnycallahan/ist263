Team Awards

These awards recognize the success of entire teams in the NHL.

Stanley Cup

Given to the NHL playoff champion at the end of the postseason.

  • 2025 - Florida Panthers
  • 2024 - Vegas Golden Knights
  • 2023 - Colorado Avalanche
  • 2022 - Tampa Bay Lightning
  • 2021 - Tampa Bay Lightning

Presidents Trophy

Awarded to the team with the best regular-season record.

  • 2025 - Winnipeg Jets
  • 2024 - Boston Bruins
  • 2023 - Florida Panthers
  • 2022 - Colorado Avalanche
  • 2021 - Washington Capitals

Conference Champions

Given to the teams that win each conference in the playoffs.

  • 2025 East - Florida Panthers | West - Edmonton Oilers
  • 2024 East - New York Rangers | West - Vegas Golden Knights
  • 2023 East - Florida Panthers | West - Colorado Avalanche
  • 2022 East - Tampa Bay Lightning | West - Colorado Avalanche
  • 2021 East - Tampa Bay Lightning | West - Dallas Stars
